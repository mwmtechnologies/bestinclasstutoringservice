About us

Thabo Chauke

Co-founder

Thabo’s passion for mathematics and science drives the academic structure at Best In Class. He believes that every learner can excel with the right support.

Mlanga Mlangisto

Co-founder

Mlanga’s expertise in accounting, economics, and business studies turns complex subjects into practical knowledge. He co-founded Best In Class to uplift the community.

Our story: Thabo and Mlanga met at the University of Johannesburg. While tutoring fellow students, they saw a gap: many high school learners in Kagiso lacked affordable weekend support. Together, they built Best In Class – a place where confidence in education grows, and every learner is empowered to succeed.
